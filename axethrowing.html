Using Unreal Engine, C/C++, and Pendulum Motion, I made a game recreating the web-swinging, web-zipping, and wall-crawling of SpiderMan in a smaller fashion without any fight actions or animations.

Disclaimer: The project is more of a demonstration of my physics knowledge combined with my game development. This game is not particularly polished and does have some bugs.
